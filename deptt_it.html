Department of Information Technology

Introduction

The web has changed the way the world looks at information. The expending role of I.T in business, science, government, social structure and personal lines is obvious in the current generation. As an academic discipline, I.T focuses on meeting the needs of users within an organizational & societal context through the selection, creation, application, integration and administration of computing technologies.

Information Technology has placed India on global map and excellence in this field has given a leading edge. Most of the I.T. clients are located abroad. Therefore, the students opting for this branch have to not only become experts in their respective field but to understand that the work calls for proper handling and interacting with foreign clients. Therefore English language is given stress to develop communication skills. Group discussions, quizzes, debates, presentations, seminars are a regular feature. Games, Sports and extra curricular competitions are held to develop their talents. Personality Development Programs are run regularly by external agencies. Experienced faculty pays personal attention to each and every student. In addition to University specified syllabi, the students are exposed to latest emerging areas to make them employ worthy Learning and teaching methodology is adopted to suit every student individually. All this aims to develop an outstanding engineering graduate with all round personality and employability. Regular lectures by guest and visiting faculty from industry and relevant fields give an additional advantage to the students. Standards books of good publishers give them wider exposure. Improvement is taken as a continuous process for better results. Thrust areas and key result areas of the I.T. Department are well defined and dynamically modified as per requirements. LKCE was established in 2006. But B.Tech. in I.T branch started one year later, from academic session 2007-08 with an intake of 60 students and increased to 90 from 2008-09.
