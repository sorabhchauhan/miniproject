Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Introduction

Electrical and Electronics Engineering is an existential stream which helps its students to variegate their abilities by encouraging critical thinking by introducing out of box learning techniques.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering branch prepares students to specialize in key areas of Electronics and related aspects of Electrical Engineering. It provides ample opportunities for students in industries dealing with Consumer Electronics, Power Electronics, Instrumentation and controls, Power supplies for various electronic and electric equipments/ measuring instruments. Since students are also given a fair amount of ideas in electrical machines and power systems and protections, so opportunities are also available in these areas.
