Training & Placement

CICDU-Corporate Interface & Career Development Unit


Lord Krishna Group of Institutions has been successful in adding more and more companies in the list of its recruiters. CICDU tries to bridge the gap between analytical and functional skills by providing the best fit to the students. Our students are ready to undertake any challenge from the first day of their professional life. The cross-sectoral participation in the campus placements bears testimony to the quality of education and excellent talent pool of Lord Krishna Group of Institutions.

As the name depicts, this Center was established to facilitate a proactive interaction between the academia and corporate world. The continuous efforts of CICDU comprising of experienced members of faculty, business development officers, selected student managers and under the guidance of the Executive Director and the Director General, has built a fruitful and long lasting relationship with various organizations.

As we at CICDU perennially strive to become a leader in our chosen field, we have not forgotten our responsibilities to the corporate and society at large. There is a relentless effort to facilitate the students for job opportunities existing in the market for budding business navigators and narrow the gap between the corporate houses and the institution. Undoubtedly our competitors may have access to the same technologies that we have, but our success lies in our ability to superimpose contemporary framework onto a Master Plan for the future.

The calm serene and hassle free ambience of LKGI enthuses its students to imbibe the humanistic and egalitarian social ethos. The process is further sharpened by our institutional modules which equip the students with skills to shoulder the real world challenges.

The activity of CICDU is supported by associations and bodies such as CSI, FICCI, CII, GMA, DMA and NHRDN.

The Center is also responsible for conducting various events like MDPs, personality development programs for the student, guest lectures, industry visits, seminars & conferences, participation in education and career fairs and networking. The PDPs are organized to enhance their soft skills and provide an edge in the selection process.

Training Activities

Organisation of CICDU

The Corporate Interface and Career Development Unit of LKGI has in it responsible and experienced members who look after the interests of the students in a way that they are ready to face the challenges of the corporate world without buckling under pressure.

Personality Development Cell (PDC)

Value Enhancement Cell (VEC)

Corporate-Interface Centre for Development Unit (CICDU) activities can be broadly categorized into

• Industry Interface and Industry Visits
• Summer Training Placement
• Pre-Placement Talks and Final Placement
• Guest Lectures, Seminars & Conferences
• Skill set enhancement
• Training of students in value added courses like CAD, Embedded Technologies, VLSI,VHDL, .Net, J2EE, CATIA, Pro-E, SQL, Automation, Robotics.

Personality Development Cell (PDC)
This cell is the backbone of the department , because we believe that unless the ore is cut and polished it is not called a Diamond, and that is why we aim at improving the communication skills of the student so that they are ready to interact with their superiors , peers and competitors with ease. Our responsibilities include:

• Soft Skills training
• English Language training, especially for students from rural and vernacular backgrounds.
