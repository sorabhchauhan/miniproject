Department of Computer Science

Introduction

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is one of the streams that is much solicited by students pursuing their B.Tech. It is renowned for imparting state of the art education and preparing its students for the challenges of the Industrial world. The curriculum of the department provides a strong foundation in all areas of Computer Science and Engineering. The aim of the department is to provide high quality education with a blend of high quality experiments, to produce leading edge quality professionals. It is one of the recognized campuses in NCR region. The objective of the department is to induce research for improved learning via Industrial visits, Technical debates, In-house job oriented training and Out side expertise training. We emphasize on hard work, discipline, motivation and excellence.

HOD Profile
Mr. S.K. Tyagi is a renowned professional in the area of computer science and engineering. He has a vast experience in teaching and industry. He has worked with organizations like Ministry of Planning, Mewar institute and IPEM before joining LKGI. He has more than 28 years of experience. He has carried out many projects in the domain of billing, production, tax, inventory and Pay roll etc. He has organized several National Level Seminars and events. He has taught subjects like Computer Networks, Data Structures, Operating System, Computer Organization, Unix/Linux, C-Programming, Linear Programming, System Analysis and Design and Software Engineering etc. In addition to that he has good administrative capabilities. He has good network in IT industry. His past students are working at hi-profile levels in the IT industry.

COMPUTER LAB

The department is equipped with various laboratories including the computer laboratory, which provides all the computational facilities ranging from computing area to high-end programming and internet access round the clock. The air conditioned computer laboratory has sufficient number of latest core 2-duo computers, all extensively networked with a rich collection of software. Internet facility through dedicated lease lines enables the students to reach further beyond the information given in the books. The Department has licensed software good enough to take care of day to day needs of the students in various labs.
