Department of Applied Science and Humanities

Introduction

Department Of Applied and Humanities is the backbone of engineering system. It consists of five different fields of human knowledge which are:

Physics,
Mathematics,
Chemistry,
Professional Communication,
Environment and Ecology


Physics
Is conceptual and theoretical ladder for an engineer. The knowledge pf Physics gives conceptual understanding to engineers in the field of Electronics and communication, Mechanical Engineering and Electrical Engineering etc.

Physics Laboratory
To understand the theoretical aspect we have a well equipped Laboratory which enables to verify the Physics Law. We have most dedicated faculty members.

Mathematics
Mathematics is a conceptual tool to utilize and formulating the problem of any engineering discipline. A basic engineering knowledge is of utmost important to any engineer. We have reputed faculty members who are involved to nurture and upgrade the basic of mathematics of upcoming engineers.

Chemistry
Chemistry apprises the students with fundamental knowledge of chemical reaction and their application and utilization in fields of engineering.

Chemistry Laboratory
To understand the concept we have a well equipped Laboratory which enables to give insight view of material and their physical state through experiments.

Professional Communication
Professional Communication is a training which engineers the career the engineers through improving the communication skills and inculcates their personalities with confidence.

Professional Communication Lab
TO TRAIN AND IMPROVE COMMUNICATION SKILL OF THE STUDENTS HAVE A WELL EQUIPPED COMMUNICATION LAB WITH STAKE OF THE ART SOFTWARE.

Environment and Ecology
It makes the students aware of the environment and the interaction between humans and industries. It also trains them to implement the technical skill without effecting environments and do their bit for sustainable developments.
