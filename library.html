Library

MISSION
To provide continuous access to the Knowledge and Information in the Library to the students and faculty of the Institute for achieving excellence in their chosen disciplines.

General Information:

The libraries at LKGI occupy over 40,000 square feet of area and provide access to a vast repository of resources, including books and periodicals to students and faculty. Our Central library is air-conditioned and well lit to provide ideal study environment. Libraries remain open until 10.00 PM on all days. During the examinations, libraries are open for nearly 24 hours. Completely automated library management system makes it possible to search, select and borrow books in no time as well as make reservations online. Multiple copies ensure that resources are easily available for reference in the library. Trained staff is always at hand to assist students. In addition to these resources, faculty members dynamically upload all their lectures and research notes on the LKGI Intranet. These are available to the students with the simple keying in of a password.

Internet facility is also available with in library areas.
